Fresh Summer Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 0 mins | Total Time: 15 mins
Course: Appetizer/Side | Cuisine: International | Diet: Vegan/Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, romaine)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup sunflower seeds or walnuts
  • ½ cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • ½ tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare vegetables: Wash and chop all vegetables as indicated.
  2. Make dressing: Whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey/maple syrup, Dijon mustard, salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble salad: In a large bowl, combine greens, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and red onion.
  4. Add toppings: Sprinkle with sunflower seeds/walnuts and feta cheese (if using).
  5. Dress and serve: Drizzle with dressing just before serving and toss gently.
Serves: 4 | Calories per serving: ~250

Chef's Tips

  • For extra protein, add grilled chicken or chickpeas
  • Make it vegan by omitting the feta cheese
  • Add seasonal fruits like strawberries or pears for sweetness
  • Prepare dressing separately and add just before eating to keep greens crisp
