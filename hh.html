Fluffy Pancakes

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 25 mins
Course: Breakfast | Cuisine: American | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ¼ cups (300ml) milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter (plus extra for cooking)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup and fresh berries to serve

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  2. Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter and vanilla.
  3. Make batter: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined (small lumps are okay).
  4. Rest batter: Let sit for 5 minutes while heating your pan/griddle.
  5. Cook pancakes: Heat a lightly buttered pan over medium heat. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form on surface (2-3 mins), then flip and cook until golden (1-2 mins).
  6. Serve: Stack warm pancakes with maple syrup and fresh berries.
Makes: 8-10 pancakes | Calories per serving (2 pancakes): ~300

Pro Tips for Fluffier Pancakes

  • Don't overmix the batter - lumps are good for fluffy texture
  • Let the batter rest to activate the baking powder
  • Use room temperature ingredients for even mixing
  • Cook on medium heat - too hot will burn outside before inside cooks
  • Try substituting buttermilk for regular milk for extra tenderness
