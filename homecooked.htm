Fresh Summer Salad

A refreshing mix of seasonal vegetables with homemade dressing

Vegetarian Gluten Free 25 min Easy

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. 1 Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. 2 In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  3. 3 In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. 4 Drizzle dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. 5 Serve immediately and enjoy!
