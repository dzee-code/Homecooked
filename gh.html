Creamy Garlic Pasta

Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins | Total Time: 20 mins
Course: Main | Cuisine: Italian | Diet: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine or linguine
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • ½ cup (50g) freshly grated Parmesan + extra for serving
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta: Boil in salted water until al dente. Reserve ¼ cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté garlic: Melt butter over medium-low heat. Cook garlic 1 min (do not brown).
  3. Make sauce: Add cream, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Simmer 3–4 mins until thickened.
  4. Combine: Toss pasta in sauce, adding pasta water as needed for silkiness.
  5. Garnish: Sprinkle parsley and extra Parmesan.
Serves: 2 | Calories per serving: ~750
